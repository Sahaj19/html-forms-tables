<table>

#Major Components of a Table
caption - Title of our table(contained inside of <caption></caption> tag).
thead - the header of our table(contained inside of <thead></thead> tag).
tbody - the body of our table(contained inside of <tbody></tbody> tag).
tfoot - the footer of our body(contained inside of <tfoot></tfoot> tag).


#Content Fillers of our table.
th - used to display table header(represented by <th></th>).
tr - used to display table row(represented by <tr></tr>).
td - used to display table data(represented by <td></td>).


#Important Attributes.
colspan & rowspan - used to create cells which spans over multiple columns and rows respectively.
default setting : rowspan = "1" & colspan = "1"

</table>
